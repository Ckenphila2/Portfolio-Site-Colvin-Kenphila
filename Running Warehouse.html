Running Warehouse

Running Warehouse is an online specialty retailer that sells running shoes, apparel, and gear, known for its large selection, expert reviews, and runner-focused fit advice. I led the redesign of brand pages, Shop All pages, and the Video Vault, improving structure, usability, and overall user experience.


Key Learnings & Results

  • Moving from Text to Image based navigation.

    • Redesigning each page a common theme was the focusing on Imaged based navigation instead of a general product dump page. This improved on customer engagement and made navigation easier and more mobile friendly.

  • Focus on Mobile center ideas.

    • 85% of Running Warehouse sales is from mobile sales rather than a traditional desktop, the designs that I created incorperated a more mobile friendly focus.

  • Product Dump pages do not work.

    • We found that product dump pages were not a good way to retain customers. These pages were hard to navigate and we found that customers would get "scoll" fatigue, leading to designs that were easier to find and use.

Original Design of Running Warehouse

Much of Running Warehouse brand pages are dumps with products underneath. These pages did not offer much in the way of navigation, and each product "type" was seperated using h3 text.

First Updated design

In partner ship with management, We did a initial redesign of each brand's page with the tools provide through the proprietaty system Running Warehouse uses called SalesMaker. This system allow us to create categories for easier search and navigation on each brand page. however these main pages still acted as a product dump page. while a great idea for the individual shoe and apparel pages, this designed did not work well for customer engagement on the brands main page.

Figma design

On my own, I began designing the next interation of the portal page with managements approval to resdesign that new page for a more tailored look that is more engaging with customers on both mobile and computer.
