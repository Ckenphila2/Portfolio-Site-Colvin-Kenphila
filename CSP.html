CSP: Conversation Starter Pack

The Conversation Starter Pack was an idea from an entrepreneur for connecting stranges and new friends. CSP is more than just a card gameâ€”it is a social experience designed to bring people closer together. Built around meaningful prompts and deep conversation starters, every card in CSP helps players open up, share stories, and connect on a personal level.


Key Learnings & Results

  • Simlpicity is Key.

    • Tested and desiged with influences from apple, the website was designed to spark a sense of an easy approach to connecting with others. Simple designs are needed and over-complications just lead to murky waters.

  • Focus on building story.

    • Through prototyping, build a cohesieve story in important for building report and trust. Getting on a personal level with customers creates a connection with the product.

  • Simple navigation reduce navigation fatigue.

    • All on a single page, the design of the website allows views to focus more on the idea of CSP rather than navigating page to page.
